# StudyVibe 📚

## Overview
StudyVibe is an interactive web-based study platform designed to solve one of the biggest problems students face: lack of focus, motivation, and consistency while studying.

Many students struggle with procrastination, distraction, and studying alone for long periods, which makes studying stressful and boring. StudyVibe transforms the studying experience into a more motivating, productive, and enjoyable journey through collaborative study rooms, focus tools, break activities, and progress tracking.

The platform creates a comfortable study environment where students can focus, communicate, organize their goals, and stay motivated while studying individually or with others.

---

# Problem Statement 💡

Traditional studying methods often feel:
- Boring and repetitive
- Isolating for students
- Difficult to stay consistent with
- Full of distractions
- Lacking motivation and interaction

StudyVibe was built to solve these problems by creating a study-focused social environment that combines productivity, interaction, organization, and fun.

# Features 🚀

## 👤 User System
- User registration and login system
- Personalized user profiles
- Secure session management
- Individual study tracking

---

## 🏠 Study Rooms

### 🌍 Public Rooms
Public rooms allow students to study together in a collaborative environment where users can interact and motivate each other.

Features:
- Group studying
- Shared study atmosphere
- Real-time interaction
- Live communication between users

### 🔒 Private Rooms
Private rooms provide a quieter and more focused studying experience for users who prefer studying alone or with close friends.

Features:
- More privacy
- Better concentration
- Comfortable focused environment

---

## 💬 Real-Time Chat System
StudyVibe includes a fully functional real-time chat system inside study rooms.

Features:
- Live messaging between users
- Instant communication during study sessions
- Encourages collaboration and motivation
- Makes studying feel more interactive and less isolating

---

## ⏳ Focus Timer & Break System
The platform includes a study timer system that helps students stay productive and organized during study sessions.

Features:
- Focus study sessions
- Break sessions between studying periods
- Productivity support similar to the Pomodoro technique
- Helps users maintain concentration and avoid burnout

---

## 🎮 Break Mini Game
To make breaks more enjoyable and reduce stress, StudyVibe includes a mini game that users can play during break time.

The game helps students:
- Relax mentally between study sessions
- Refresh their energy and focus
- Reduce boredom during long study periods
- Return to studying with better concentration

After the break ends, a special alert appears reminding users to return to studying and continue their focus session.

---

## 🎯 Focus Planner
StudyVibe includes a daily focus planner that helps users organize their study goals and tasks.

Features:
- Add daily goals and tasks
- Mark completed tasks
- Remove unnecessary tasks
- Improve productivity and organization
- Stay focused on daily objectives

---

## 📊 Profile & Progress Tracking
Users have access to a personalized profile dashboard that tracks their studying progress over time.

Features:
- Weekly study progress chart
- Study heatmap
- Study distribution statistics
- Total focus hours
- Session counter
- Study streak tracking
- Motivational study status system

This helps students monitor their consistency and stay motivated.

StudyVibe focuses on creating a visually comfortable and motivating studying environment.

Features:
